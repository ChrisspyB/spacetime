Canvas 4: Twins "Paradox": a hidden asymmetry
Earth Frame:
  • Time between departure and direction change: 5 - 0 = 5yr.
  • Time between direction change and arrival: 10 - 5 = 5yr.
    • Total time elapsed on earth: 5 + 5 = 10yr.

    Spaceship Departure Frame:
  • Time between departure and direction change: 3 - 0 = 3yr.
  • Time between direction change and arrival has no meaning in this frame.
    • Spaceship Return Frame:
  • Time between departure and direction change has no meaning in this frame.
  • Time between direction change and arrival: 16.67 - 13.67 = 3yr.
    • Total time elapsed on ship: 3 + 3 = 6yr.

    → Twin on spaceship arrives 4 years younger than twin sibling on earth.
